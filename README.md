Hey Curious Mind ,
This project is visioned to build a platfrom for creative people having intrest or skills , from basic to master level to put their skills to work in a productive manner , by contributing in real projects , by collaborating with contemporary fellowcreators skilled in similar or distinct domains .

Eg :
- Say  "A" is a musician , "B" is intrested in writing songs , "C" is being trained in singing . CoCreate helps them to find each other and work on the project , and also put their work online for the world , and community to discover and appretiate them and their talent .

Features envisioned as of now :
- Discover : people put their works / projects here so others can see it .
- Collab : here people can see projects that others are working on and can apply to contribute .
- Post Projects : people put out their on-going / envisioned projects here .
- Premium Subscription : helps us (the platform get funds) and the users use more special features .

This is in short : Linkedin , GitHub and Instagram for Creatives (film-making realted as of now).
